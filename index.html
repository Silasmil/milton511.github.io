About Me

I’m Milton Silas, a cybersecurity analyst, red team specialist, and Cisco IT Essentials instructor. I bridge the gap between technical security expertise and teaching clarity.

My work revolves around threat detection, incident response, vulnerability assessment, and penetration testing. I build hands-on security labs and workshops to help learners move from theory to practice, making cybersecurity skills accessible to beginners and professionals alike.

Beyond security, I integrate design and content creation into my training — creating visuals, walkthroughs, and interactive labs that make complex topics easier to understand and apply in real-world scenarios.

My goal is simple: empower others to learn, practice, and grow in cybersecurity while shaping a safer digital space.
