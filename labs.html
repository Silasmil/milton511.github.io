Hack The Box · Introduction to Networking

Completed: October 14, 2025 · Tools: Wireshark, Nmap, Aircrack-ng

Problem Statement

Understand core networking models and apply packet analysis, subnetting and protocol inspection to identify weaknesses and hardening steps.

Approach

  1. Reviewed OSI and TCP/IP models and mapped lab tasks to layers.
  2. Captured traffic in Wireshark and analysed PDUs to identify anomalies.
  3. Performed subnetting exercises and designed segmentation examples.
  4. Tested wireless security with Aircrack-ng in controlled lab environment.

Tools Used

  • Wireshark
  • Nmap
  • Aircrack-ng

Key Findings & Lessons

  • Segmentation prevents lateral movement. Proper /25 or /27 planning reduces exposure.
  • ARP spoofing can be mitigated with DAI and static ARP entries.
  • Wireless audits must include both configuration checks and handshake captures for robust assessment.

TryHackMe · Python Basics
